# Privacy Policy for PyGrounds

**Last Updated:** May 22, 2026

At PyGrounds ("we", "our", or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, **PyGrounds** (the "App"), which is designed to help users learn Python and Artificial Intelligence.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Data
When you sign in to PyGrounds using Google Sign-In, we collect certain personally identifiable information to establish and manage your learning account:
*   **Name & Profile Picture**: Provided by your identity provider (Google) to customize your app experience.
*   **Email Address**: Used to uniquely identify your account, sync your learning data across multiple devices, and communicate essential updates.
*   **User ID (`uid`)**: A unique identifier generated by Firebase Authentication to secure and reference your data in our database.

### B. App Progression & Usage Data
To deliver a personalized educational experience, we save and update your course progress:
*   **Completed Lessons**: Tracks which tutorials you have finished.
*   **Quiz Scores**: Logs your results on module assessments.
*   **Practice Lab Submissions**: Saves your code progress in the interactive programming labs.
*   *Note: This data is stored locally on your device via `AsyncStorage` and periodically synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore for backup and multi-device access.*

### C. Device and Push Notification Data
If you enable **Study Reminders** (daily learning notifications), we collect:
*   **FCM Registration Token**: A unique device identifier generated by Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver reminders.
*   **Timezone & Preferences**: Your selected reminder interval (e.g., Morning, Lunch, Evening, Night) and device timezone to trigger notifications at the correct local time.

---

## 2. Interactive Features & On-Device Data Handling

### A. On-Device Python Engine
PyGrounds features an interactive **Practice Lab** that executes Python code. 
*   **All code execution takes place entirely on your local device** using our built-in client-side Javascript compilation engine.
*   We **do not** transmit your playground code, scripts, or raw variables to any remote servers for execution or storage.

### B. Third-Party PDF Viewer
The App displays hosted textbook resources using a secure `WebView`.
*   These documents are loaded from public cloud repositories (such as Google Drive).
*   The WebView respects standard browser security protocols. No personal identifier data is shared with the file-hosting server.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
1.  **To Provide and Maintain the Service**: Storing your progress so you can resume learning exactly where you left off.
2.  **To Manage Your Account**: Authenticating your identity through Google Sign-In.
3.  **To Send Study Reminders**: Dispatching daily reminders using Firebase Cloud Messaging based on your preferred time slot.
4.  **To Optimize Performance**: Improving the learning interface and resolving code engine runtime errors.

---

## 4. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

**We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.** 

We only share information with trusted third-party service providers to perform actions necessary to operate the App:
*   **Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Messaging)**: Used to authenticate users, store progress data securely, and send push notifications. All data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policies.
*   **Legal Obligations**: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal processes or protect the rights and safety of our users.

---

## 5. Security of Your Data

The security of your personal data is important to us. We use secure cloud databases (Google Firebase) that employ industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and at rest. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 6. Your Data Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:
*   **Access & Copy**: You can view your current progress, account profile, and preferences directly within the App interface.
*   **Data Deletion**: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated synchronized progress. To do so, contact us at the email address provided below.
*   **Opt-Out of Push Notifications**: You can disable daily Study Reminders at any time directly through the App settings or by blocking notifications in your Android system settings.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this data from our servers.
