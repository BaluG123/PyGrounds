# PyGrounds Product Roadmap

The goal is to make PyGrounds a serious offline-first learning app, not just a content browser.

## Version 1.0 Goal

Ship a stable first version that helps students learn Python and AI basics through lessons, quizzes, practice, PDFs, and playground labs.

Version 1.0 should be reliable, clear, and useful even without internet.

## What Makes the App Valuable

- Students can open the app anytime and solve small problems.
- Lessons explain concepts in simple language.
- Practice labs turn passive reading into active learning.
- Progress tracking gives a sense of movement.
- PDF notes support revision.
- Offline-first behavior helps students with limited connectivity.

## High-Impact Features After Version 1

1. Daily Challenge

Show one problem per day with streak tracking.

2. Bookmarks

Let students save hard questions and revisit them.

3. Search

Search courses, lessons, concepts, and practice questions.

4. Stronger Playground

Replace the current training runner with a fuller Python runtime when feasible.

5. Offline PDF Downloads

Allow students to download PDFs for later reading.

6. Progress Dashboard

Show completed lessons, solved labs, quiz scores, and weak topics.

7. Hindi and Regional Language Support

For Indian students, selective multilingual support could be a major advantage.

8. Exam and Career Tracks

Add tracks for:

- Python beginner
- Data analyst
- Machine learning beginner
- AI project portfolio
- College exam revision

## Product Principles

- Every screen should answer: what should the student do next?
- Prefer practice over long theory.
- Keep explanations short, visual, and actionable.
- Make offline behavior dependable.
- Do not add features that make the app fragile before release.

## Version 1 Launch Message

PyGrounds helps students build programming confidence through small daily practice, clear AI foundations, and an offline-first learning experience.
